Welcome to TwinCoder.org

TwinCoder.org is a free tool to teach kids how to code. It resembles traditional programing languages and it has a development interface that is simple to use.

You can find new versions, more examples and videos with instructions at http://twincoder.org. You can also subscribe to our Twitter account https://twitter.com/twincoder to see what's going on in the community.
